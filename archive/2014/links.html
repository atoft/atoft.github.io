Gaming

LambdaGeneration.com

Since Summer 2013, I have been an editor of the Modding and Development section of LambdaGeneration.com, a leading Valve Community fansite. Check it out for news, reviews and discussion of maps and mods for Valve games.

PlanetPhillip

A comprehensive database of maps and mods for Valve games. Phillip also gives away free games, runs mapping competitions and lots more. There is also a unique reviews system to help you find good mods and give your opinion. PlanetPhillip has a really nice community and it is fun to take part.

Steam Forums

A place for discussing games but don't take things too seriously!

Eurogamer

A good all-round gaming information site.
