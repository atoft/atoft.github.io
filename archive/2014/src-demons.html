"These Peculiar Demons"
"For it was said they had become like these peculiar demons, which dwell in matter but in whom no light may be found."

A map created as part of the RavenholmVille level design competition for Half-Life 2: Episode 2. Search for survivors in a Zombie infested town and hope that you can survive the night.
