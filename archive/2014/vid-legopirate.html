LEGO: The Pirate Treasure
A humble LEGO pirate wants nothing more than to find buried treasure, but his quest is interrupted by a skeleton pirate who’s out to get him!
A stop-motion animation using LEGO bricks and figures, using a DV camera and capturing still images via computer software. The music is from Disney’s Pirates of the Caribbean (however this had to be removed from YouTube due to copyright issues.

Released: October 2008 (original release date)
