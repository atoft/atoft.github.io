About Me
Hi, I'm Alastair. I make things with computers!

I'm a second year Computer Scientist at the University of Cambridge. When I'm not really busy with my course I'll work on my own projects, whether that's web design, programming, or making games, maps and mods.

AddyMotion.com is my strangely named personal website where you can find some of my stuff, follow my blog, or get in touch.

Contact me for a copy of my CV.

This is a brand new website, so if something goes wrong feel free to let me know!
